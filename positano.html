Explore the Wonder of Positano

By Lina Fowler

On my trip to the Amalfi Coast I could have never imagined the wonder and beauty I would be exposed to. It really gave me a surreal feeling to be there and soak it all in. Positano is more beautiful than I could ever have imagined. Also, never have I ever seen so many colorful shoes! There are several things to do in Positano. One of the main things people enjoy doing is shopping. Positano is known for its leather shoes.

"Never have I ever seen so many colorful shoes!"

We stopped at multiple little shops as we enjoyed this beautiful little city. Almost every shop we went to had a number of different kinds of shoes. All in all we had a wonderful time.
